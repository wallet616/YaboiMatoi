About

Fabian "YaboiMatoi" Piwowarczyk, (born August 2nd, 1998) is a guitarist and composer of heavily guitar-driven instrumentals.

He plays Electric Guitar, Bass Guitar and Classical Guitar. With his influences ranging from mainly Video-Game-Music to Metal, Prog, Rock and Jazz he records, mixes and masters his own tracks at his small, modest home studio based in Germany.

After first picking up the guitar in April 2014, he has been looking up to players such as FamilyJules, John Petrucci, Dimebag Darrell, Synyster Gates and composer Danny Baranowsky. His first guitar being a Schecter SGR, he continues to use a Schecter Hellraiser C-1 FR as his main instrument of choice.

While still keeping music a priority, he enjoys spending his free time playing games such as The Binding of Isaac, Super Meat Boy, Crypt of the Necrodancer, Rocket League and many more.
